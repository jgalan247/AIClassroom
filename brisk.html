Brisk Teaching

Chrome Extension for Time-Saving AI Teaching Tools

What is Brisk Teaching?

Brisk Teaching is a powerful Chrome extension that integrates AI-powered features directly into your browser. It allows you to generate quizzes, feedback, lesson plans, and differentiated materials from any webpage or document you're viewing‚Äîwith just a few clicks.

Simply highlight text on any website, click the Brisk button, and instantly create educational resources. The extension works seamlessly with Google Docs, news articles, educational websites, and more, making it incredibly fast and convenient for busy teachers.

üí° How It Works

Install the Chrome extension, then while browsing any webpage:
1. Highlight the text you want to work with
2. Click the Brisk icon in your browser
3. Choose what you want to create (quiz, worksheet, lesson plan, etc.)
4. Brisk instantly generates the resource for you
5. Review, edit if needed, and use in your classroom!
