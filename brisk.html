What is Brisk Teaching?

Brisk Teaching is a Chrome extension that integrates AI-powered features directly into your browser. It allows you to generate quizzes, feedback, lesson plans, and more from any webpage or document you're viewing. The prompts below show you how to achieve similar results using standalone AI tools.
