üí° Tips for Effective Note-Taking and Revision

  • Synthesize, Don't Copy: Combine information from multiple sources in your own words
  • Create Connections: Link new information to what you already know
  • Use Active Recall: Test yourself regularly instead of just re-reading notes
  • Make It Visual: Use diagrams, mind maps, and flowcharts to represent information
  • Spaced Repetition: Review notes at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks)
  • Practice Questions: Always include practice questions with your notes
  • Identify Weak Spots: Use AI to generate extra practice on topics you struggle with
  • Keep It Concise: Revision notes should be summaries, not essays
  • Update Regularly: Add exam tips and common mistakes as you discover them
