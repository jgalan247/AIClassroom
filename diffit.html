What is Diffit?

Diffit is an AI-powered differentiation tool that automatically adapts educational resources to meet diverse learner needs. It takes any text, article, or topic and creates multiple versions at different reading levels‚Äîcomplete with comprehension questions, vocabulary support, and visual aids.

Whether you're supporting struggling readers, challenging advanced students, or creating resources for EAL learners, Diffit makes differentiation fast and effortless. Simply input your content or topic, choose your grade levels, and Diffit generates everything you need in seconds.

üí° How It Works

Option 1 - From a Topic: Enter a topic (e.g., "photosynthesis"), select grade level and reading levels, and Diffit creates differentiated articles with questions

Option 2 - From Your Text: Paste in an article or text you want to use, and Diffit adapts it to multiple reading levels while preserving key content

Option 3 - From a URL: Provide a link to an online article, and Diffit extracts and differentiates the content automatically
