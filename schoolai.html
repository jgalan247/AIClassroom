What is School AI?

School AI is a UK-focused educational AI platform specifically designed for schools with built-in safeguarding features, curriculum alignment, and age-appropriate content filters. Unlike general AI tools, School AI is tailored for the UK education system with GDPR compliance and safety features that make it suitable for student use.

The platform provides a safe environment where students can ask questions, get explanations, and receive support with their learning‚Äîall while teachers maintain oversight and control.
